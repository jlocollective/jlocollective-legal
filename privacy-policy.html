This Privacy Policy describes how JLO Collective ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information you provide when you interact with our real estate services and communications, including SMS text messaging.

01

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you submit an inquiry, contact form, or sign-up sheet, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Phone number (mobile or landline)
  • Email address
  • Home-buying timeline, budget, and preferences
  • Any message or notes you include in your inquiry

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website or interact with our digital content, we may collect:

  • IP address and general location
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Referring source (how you found us)

Information from Third-Party Platforms

If you inquire through third-party real estate platforms (such as Zillow, Realtor.com, or Facebook), we may receive the contact information and inquiry details you provided on those platforms.

02

SMS & Text Message Communications

📱 SMS Consent Notice

By providing your phone number and submitting an inquiry form, you expressly consent to receive automated and manually-sent SMS text messages from JLO Collective regarding real estate services, property information, and appointment scheduling. Consent is not a condition of purchase. Message and data rates may apply.

What We Send

We may send SMS messages containing:

  • Initial response to your real estate inquiry
  • Follow-up messages regarding properties or appointments
  • Scheduling confirmations and reminders
  • Informational updates about available homes or promotions
  • Responses to your inbound messages

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your inquiry and engagement. You may receive up to 10 messages per month. We do not send unsolicited bulk marketing messages.

How to Opt Out

You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any text message. After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message and no further SMS messages will be sent. You may also opt out by emailing us at jlo.collective@gmail.com.

Getting Help

Reply HELP to any message to receive assistance, or contact us at jlo.collective@gmail.com.

Carrier Disclaimer

Message and data rates may apply. JLO Collective is not responsible for any charges incurred from your mobile carrier related to receiving SMS messages.

03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your real estate inquiry promptly
  • Schedule and confirm property tours or consultations
  • Send SMS messages you have consented to receive
  • Maintain a record of our communications with you
  • Improve our services and communication quality
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations, including TCPA
  • Prevent fraud and protect the security of our systems

We do not use your information for automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects without human involvement.

04

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist us in delivering services (e.g., SMS delivery platforms, CRM systems). These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and may not use your data for their own purposes.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of business assets, with appropriate notice to you.
  • With Your Consent: For any other purpose, only with your explicit consent.

Our SMS communications are facilitated through Twilio, Inc., a third-party SMS platform. Your phone number is transmitted to Twilio solely for the purpose of message delivery and is not shared with other Twilio customers.

05

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Specifically:

  • Active lead records: retained while your inquiry is open and for up to 2 years after last contact
  • Opt-out records: retained indefinitely to ensure we honor your opt-out preference
  • Communication logs: retained for up to 3 years for compliance and legal purposes

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us, subject to any legal obligations that require retention.

06

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-Out of SMS: Reply STOP to any text message at any time
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at jlo.collective@gmail.com. We will respond to verified requests within 30 days.

07

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include encrypted data transmission, access controls, and secure cloud infrastructure.

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

08

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

09

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our services after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
