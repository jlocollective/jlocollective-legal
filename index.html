Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026 · Last Updated: March 8, 2026

1. Overview

SIGND.AI ("we," "us," or "our") is a software platform operated by JLO Collective that provides automated real estate lead response and follow-up services for licensed real estate agents. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information in connection with our services.

By using our platform or receiving communications facilitated through our system, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Full name and contact information (phone number, email address)
  • Property inquiry details submitted through real estate platforms
  • SMS and communication history between leads and agents
  • Device and usage data when interacting with our platform
  • Agent account information including name, brokerage, and credentials

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Sending automated and agent-approved SMS follow-up messages to real estate inquiries
  • Managing lead pipelines and sales sequences on behalf of real estate agents
  • Improving AI response quality and platform performance
  • Complying with legal obligations including TCPA and carrier regulations
  • Providing platform analytics and reporting to agents

4. SMS Messaging & Opt-Out

End users who submit inquiries through this form consent to receive SMS communications regarding their property inquiry. Every message includes opt-out instructions.

To stop receiving messages at any time, reply STOP to any SMS. Your number will be immediately added to our opt-out list and no further messages will be sent.

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on inquiry activity.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with:

  • Licensed real estate agents using our platform to respond to your inquiry
  • Twilio Inc. for SMS delivery services
  • Anthropic for AI message generation (no PII retained by Anthropic)
  • Infrastructure providers (Railway, cloud hosting) under data processing agreements
  • Law enforcement or legal process when required by law

6. Data Retention

We retain lead and communication data for up to 24 months from the date of last activity, or as required by applicable law. Agents may request deletion of their account data at any time. Opt-out records are retained indefinitely to ensure compliance.

7. Your Rights

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@jlocollective.com.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (TLS), access controls, and secure cloud infrastructure. While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no system is completely secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page.
